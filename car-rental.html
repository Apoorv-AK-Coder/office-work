FAQs

How old do I have to be to make a rental car booking?

As per most states of the U.S.A., the minimum age required for a person to make a rental car booking is 21 years. If you're under 30, you need to pay more than the average price for car rental bookings. This is usually called a young driver's fee or young person's surcharge in the car rental industry. You might be subject to a surcharge if you are above 65 years of age. If these charges get charged, the amount will be charged extra depending on the rental car company.


Should I make an advance deposit?

A majority of car rental companies ask their travelers for a deposit. The deposit amount might differ from one car Rental Company to another. Generally, luxury car rentals need a higher deposit than minivans or economy car rentals. Hence, it is advised that you bring a valid credit card to make the deposit successfully when picking up the car at the destination.


What kind of car is best for me?

The kind of car which is best for you depends on you. The kind of car that is best for you depends on different factors like the number of people you're traveling with, the budget you have set for a car rental, the type of car you are willing to drive or travel in, and also the type of places that you're planning to travel with the car you rent.
