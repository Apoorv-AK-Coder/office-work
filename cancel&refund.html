Cancellation & Refund Policy

Since we have been dealing with this business for ages, we know that there are the type of clients who book their flight tickets but either ask for some changes in it or cancel the same due to any unexpected reasons. This may lead to severe loss to our company and thus, to cope-up with such kinds of situations, we have kept our policies very simple.

The amount of almost all the flight tickets are non-refundable after 24 hours of making your online payment. Since we have no physical location, all cancellations could be made only via our customer care support and you need to follow the terms and conditions to fall under such categories to get your cancellations and refunds accepted. Such terms and conditions are:

  • You need to follow the timeline to request for flight cancellation
  • You need to inform our customer care support immediately instead of directly missing your flight
  • Following such terms and conditions means you could get some waivers if applicable or if the airlines will get agreed
