What is Black Friday?

The traditional name of Black Friday is Thanksgiving Day and in the United States, this day is celebrated every year on the fourth Thursday of November. The subsequent Monday is mainly celebrated in the United States as the Cyber Monday. The day is also known as the shopping day because of many reasons. The primary reason is the first day after the last major holiday before Christmas and also known as the unofficial commencement of the shopping season of Christmas and New Year. On Black Friday, people go shopping and they get huge deals in the market which are too much cheap.
