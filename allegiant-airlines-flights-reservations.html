Which is the cheapest day to book Allegiant flights?

Taking into account the flight type and route, Allegiant Air determines the ideal days to fly. The best days to purchase airline tickets for domestic flights are Tuesday and Wednesday. Whereas, Wednesday and Thursday are the best days to do so for international flights.


What are the hand baggage restrictions for Allegiant Air?

All Allegiant Air flights allow you to bring one carry-on bag and one personal item. The maximum dimensions for carry-on baggage, including handles and wheels, are 16 x 15 x 7 inches (LxWxH), and they must fit under the seat or in the overhead cabinet. Allowable dimensions for personal items are 16x15x7 inches (LxWxH) and a maximum weight of 25 pounds.


What is the major hub of Allegiant Airlines?

McCarran International Airport in Las Vegas serves as Allegiant Air's primary hub. Whereas, Los Angeles, Cincinnati, Bellingham, Phoenix, Fort Walton Beach, Fort Lauderdale, and Orlando are additional Allegiant Air focus hubs.


Is it possible to buy plane tickets at the airport?

It is recommended that passengers book in advance using Allegiant Air's official website or contact center. Further, based on availability, you can purchase airline tickets for same-day travel at the airport ticket counter.


What is the benefit of using the Tripflex Program?

Allegiant Air's TripFlex program allows you to make unlimited adjustments and even postpone your flight without incurring a cost of $50-75 per passenger. Passengers can use this service to make limitless changes, such as date, flight, and destination, at no cost; adjustments can be made up to one hour before departure.


Is it possible to make a reservation for unaccompanied children?

Children between the ages of fifteen and seventeen can book online for free. Children under the age of fourteen must be accompanied by an adult who is fifteen years old or older.
