Top things to do in Honolulu

Take yourself to Waikiki Beach

The most relaxing way to unwind from a busy week is at the beach. On the southern tip of the island of Oahu in Hawaii, U.S., the city of Honolulu's Waikiki area is located right on the beach. Though there are five beaches in the area, Waikiki Beach is the most well-known one.

Honolulu Zoo

A popular tourist destination, the 42-acre Honolulu Zoo is home to a variety of animals, amphibians, reptiles, and birds. The zoo is a beautiful destination to come to with your family or friends. This is because it has more than 900 types of wildlife and a unique diversity of vegetation. It is home to a variety of creatures and birds, including tortoises, Asian elephants, chimps, hippos, monkeys, and Palm Cockatoos.

Fort DeRussy Beach Park

People are constantly present at Fort DeRussy Beach Park, from the early morning aerobics class to the volleyball, swimming, and freediving in the evening. The retired elderly who enjoy visiting this area for a stroll love the seaside park. Children who like being in natural settings flock to the park in droves for picnics with their families and friends.
