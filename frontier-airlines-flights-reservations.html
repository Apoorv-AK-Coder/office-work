What is the main hub of Frontier?

American low-cost airline Frontier Airlines (F9) has its main office in Denver, Colorado.


What are the carry-on baggage regulations for Frontier Airlines?

Frontier Airlines' carry-on luggage restrictions include a 35-pound weight limit and 10-plus-16-plus-24-inch (L+W+H) maximum size.


What are the baggage rules for Frontier flights?

Passengers with Frontier Airlines bookings can bring one bag that weighs no more than 50 lbs. The maximum linear size for checked baggage is 62 inches.


How can I check in for a Frontier Flight online?

Up to 24 hours before takeoff, check in using the online check-in portal (until 60 minutes before departure). You can also see your itinerary, buy bags or seats, and print your boarding pass all from one page.

What exactly is Frontier Miles?

Is it possible to cancel flights on Frontier?
