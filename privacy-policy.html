Data Accuracy

By providing us with PI, you confirm that the information is accurate, complete, and current. Please inform us if your PI changes to ensure we maintain accurate records.

Categories of Personal Information We Collect

We may collect, use, store, and transfer various types of PI, including:

  1. Identifiers: Name, postal address, date of birth, online identifiers (IP address), and passport/ID details.
  2. Contact Data: Billing and delivery addresses, email, and phone numbers.
  3. Commercial Information: Price quotes, purchased products or services, financial data (bank account and payment card details), and transaction data.
  4. Network Activity Information: Technical data (login data, browsing history, etc.) and usage data.
  5. Geolocation Data: Country, state, or city.
  6. Audio and Electronic Information: Call records and email threads.
  7. Inferences: Profile data (preferences, behavior) and marketing data.

We also collect aggregated data for statistical purposes, which, if combined with PI, will be treated according to this policy.

Children’s Information

We do not knowingly collect PI from minors, except for travelers. If we discover that a minor has submitted PI, we will remove it. Parents or guardians of minors can contact us to delete such information.
