United Airlines Baggage Policy:

For Regular Economy Class Fare in the United Airline One Personal Item and One Carry on Item is Free in the Baggage Policy of the United Airlines. The Charges are applicable on the checked baggage / Hold luggage, Overweight and Oversize bags and Sports equipment.

Carry on Baggage Dimension with Handles and Wheels are 45 linear inches (22 x 14 x 9 in) or 115 centimeters (56 x 36 x 23 cm). Carry On baggage must be fit in the overhead bin or under the seat in front of passengers. Personal Item Dimension for Personal Item is 36 linear inches (16 x 11 x 8 in) or 89 centimeters (41 x 28 x 20 cm) and Personal item must be fit in the overhead bin or under the seat of you.
